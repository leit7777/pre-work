Leitao's Italian Cuisine is a unique restaurant serving traditional Italian foods in a beautiful setting. Whether you want our homemade pasta from Fusilli to angel hair as well as tortolini, stuffed shells, lasagna or our traditional spaghetti and meatballs, our pasta is handmade daily using only the freshest ingredients. Located in downtown Fremont New Hampshire. Enjoy a drink on our outside deck overlooking the Spiket River by our firepit. The outdoor bar serves many traditional and non-tradition spirits and beers to satisfy any pallet. Each meal comes with complimentary salad and garlic bread with or without mozarella cheese. Non of that store bought stuff. We make our own garlic bread fresh to order with only the finest Italian ingredients. We can accomodate your parties up to 20 people. Have more guests? Enjoy a private room serving guest from 20 to 100 people. We are confident your guests will walk away happy and well fed.
